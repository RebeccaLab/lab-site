## Welcome to Rebecca AI Lab

We are excited that you are considering joining the **Rebecca AI Lab** at Feng Chia University! Our laboratory is a dynamic research environment dedicated to advancing the frontiers of artificial intelligence, with particular focus on music AI, neural networks, fuzzy logic, and deep learning applications.

### What We Offer

As a member of our lab, you will have the opportunity to:

- **Engage in cutting-edge research** that contributes to the global AI research community
- **Work on open-source projects** that have real-world impact
- **Collaborate with international researchers** and participate in academic conferences
- **Develop expertise** in machine learning, deep learning, and AI applications
- **Build a strong research portfolio** through publications and project contributions
- **Access state-of-the-art computing resources** and research infrastructure

### Our Research Areas

Our lab focuses on several key research domains:

- **Music & Audio AI**: Generation, classification, and semantic understanding of music
- **Neural Networks**: Advanced architectures including GANs, GRUs, Transformers, and xLSTM
- **Fuzzy Logic Systems**: Uncertainty handling and intelligent decision-making
- **Computer Vision**: Image analysis and multimedia understanding
- **Recommender Systems**: Personalized content recommendation algorithms

## Important Expectations

### Performance Milestones

The Rebecca AI Lab maintains high standards for research excellence. We expect all members to:

- **Meet research milestones** on schedule and contribute meaningfully to ongoing projects
- **Maintain consistent progress** toward degree completion and research objectives
- **Participate actively** in lab meetings, discussions, and collaborative work
- **Produce high-quality research outputs** including publications, code contributions, and documentation
- **Demonstrate professional conduct** in all academic and research activities

### Commitment and Work Ethic

Research in our lab requires:

- **Strict adherence to deadlines** - Research projects have firm timelines that must be respected
- **Resilience in the face of challenges** - Research involves experimentation, and not all approaches will succeed
- **Willingness to learn from failure** - Setbacks are part of the research process and valuable learning opportunities
- **Self-motivation and initiative** - Successful researchers take ownership of their work
- **Time management skills** - Balancing coursework, research, and other responsibilities effectively

### Genuine Passion Required

We are looking for students who:

- **Have a genuine passion for AI and machine learning** - This field requires continuous learning and dedication
- **Are excited about research** - Not just completing assignments, but pushing boundaries
- **Enjoy problem-solving** - Research involves tackling complex, open-ended problems
- **Value collaboration** - Working effectively with team members and contributing to shared goals
- **Are committed to open science** - Contributing to open-source projects and sharing knowledge

## Is This Lab Right for You?

### This lab may be a good fit if you:

- ✅ Are genuinely passionate about AI, machine learning, and research
- ✅ Can handle strict deadlines and manage multiple priorities effectively
- ✅ View setbacks and failures as learning opportunities
- ✅ Are self-motivated and take initiative in your work
- ✅ Enjoy collaborative research and contributing to team projects
- ✅ Are committed to producing high-quality research outputs
- ✅ Can balance coursework, research, and other responsibilities

### This lab may not be the right fit if you:

- ❌ Are primarily seeking an easy path to a degree
- ❌ Struggle with meeting deadlines or managing time effectively
- ❌ Have difficulty handling failure or setbacks
- ❌ Lack genuine interest in AI and machine learning research
- ❌ Prefer working in isolation without collaboration
- ❌ Are not willing to invest significant time and effort in research
- ❌ Cannot commit to the performance standards expected in the lab

**If you find yourself identifying more with the second list, we respectfully advise that you consider other laboratories that may better align with your goals and working style.** This will help ensure your success and satisfaction during your graduate studies.

## How to Apply

If you believe the Rebecca AI Lab is the right environment for you, we encourage you to:

1. **Review our research** - Explore our [publications](/members/about-professor/#publications) and [projects](/projects/) to understand our work
2. **Learn about our members** - Get to know the [lab team](/members/) and their research interests
3. **Contact us** - Reach out to Professor Pei-Chun Lin at **peiclin@fcu.edu.tw** with:
   - Your academic background and research interests
   - Why you are interested in joining our lab
   - How your goals align with our research focus
   - Your relevant experience and skills

### Application Materials

When contacting us, please include:

- **Academic transcript** showing your coursework and grades
- **CV or resume** highlighting relevant experience
- **Statement of purpose** explaining your research interests and goals
- **Portfolio or GitHub profile** (if applicable) showcasing your projects and code
- **Any relevant publications or research experience**

## What to Expect After Joining

New lab members can expect:

- **Orientation and onboarding** to familiarize you with lab resources and expectations
- **Assignment to research projects** based on your interests and lab needs
- **Regular lab meetings** for progress updates, discussions, and collaboration
- **Mentorship and guidance** from Professor Lin and senior lab members
- **Opportunities for conference presentations** and publication authorship
- **Support for professional development** and career goals
